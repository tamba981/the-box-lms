How To Apply

Applying to THE BOX - Building Opportunities and Expertise creates a possibility for a bright future.

THE BOX equips emerging talent with the skills needed for in-demand careers, revolutionizing the future of work. Your support makes it all happen.

Apply Now

Application Overview

Applying to THE BOX is simple and straightforward. Follow the steps below to kickstart your journey to a brighter future. Make sure to prepare the required documents and understand the deadlines.

1

Complete the Online Application Form

Start by filling out the application form with accurate personal, academic, and professional information.

2

Submit Required Documents

Upload documents such as your CV, transcripts, and letters of recommendation through the portal.

3

Attend an Interview (If Required)

Some programs may require a short interview to assess your qualifications and motivation.

4

Receive Admission Decision

Our admissions team will review your application and notify you of your admission status.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the admission process?

The admission process typically includes completing an online application, submitting required documents, and potentially attending an interview (if applicable).

Do I need to take an entrance exam?

Some programs may require entrance exams. Check the program's requirements for more details.

Can I apply for financial aid?

No, THE BOX does not offer financial aid for now.
